Bobi Wine

A Ugandan Music Artist

Kyagulanyi Robert is a ugandan music Artist known by the stage name Bobi Wine and a political activist. He has had several hits over the years.
Kyagulanyi Robert is a Ugandan born and raised in the getto areas of Kampala where he got his nick name the getto president.
