Bobi Wine

A Ugandan Politician

Kyagulanyi Robert is a ugandan music Artist known by the stage name Bobi Wine and A political activist. He has had hits over the years.
Kyagulanyi Robert is a Ugandan born and raised in the getto areas of Kampala where he got his nick name 'the getto president'.
Robert Kyagulanyi is now a Ugandan politician who joins forces with the youth to protest against the injustices on human rights on the
people of Uganda and does this through his music

Things accomplished in his political journey:

    Protested against VAT on Data usage in the year 2008
    Run for presidency for the very first time to outcompete Yoseri Museveni
